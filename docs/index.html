Personal set of plots regarding the COVID-19 outbreak. Left column displays quantities in linear scale, right column in logarithmic scale. Countries can be toggled on/off by clicking in their respective legends. Double clicking a country hides all but the clicked country.

In particular I am interested in which I think it is the most reasonable first order intercountry comparison of the phenomena by looking at the amount of deaths caused by SARS-CoV-2 normalized by population size, with focus on the South American situation compared to the most affected countries worldwide.

Data is provided by the European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control (ECDC), freely available at: https://www.ecdc.europa.eu/en/publications-data/download-todays-data-geographic-distribution-covid-19-cases-worldwide.
